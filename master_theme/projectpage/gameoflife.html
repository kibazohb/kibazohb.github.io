GAME OF LIFE

Background

The game of life, a culminative project for my course 2AA4, Introduction to Software Development, at McMaster University. I decided to take this a step further and create the desktop UI.

My team and I decided to work on Africa's corruptive land ownership management system. The main idea was to leverage IBM's blockchain and Google maps API to secure a digital record of all transactions, hence preventing corrupt people from tampering with the data. I was responsible for setting up the database using Google's firebase to store customer data and also assisted on implementing Google maps API to construct a good visual representation of owned land on the map.

Special thanks to my incredible team members Guy-Jacques Isombe, Tawana Mphusu, Shreyal Shah and Olayiowola Bakare.
