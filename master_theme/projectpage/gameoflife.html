GAME OF LIFE

The Project

The game of life, a culminative project for my course 2AA4, Introduction to Software Development, at McMaster University. I decided to take this a step further and create the desktop UI.

The project was very insightful, as the game of life was created to simulate the evolution of life and its countless possibles in only 3 rules. It put my software design and object oriented programming skills to the test and I am looking forward to developing something simlar as well.
