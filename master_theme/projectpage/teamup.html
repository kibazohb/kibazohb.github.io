YORK U HACKS @YORK U

Background

YORK U HACKS (2018) was the first hackathon held at York University. The main theme was social change

As a team we decided to build a web based applicaiton, that served as a platform for local people to share and work on ideas to bring positive change towards their society. For instance, building basketball courts in a neighbourhood. Using Angular, I was able to serve a role in building the frontend for this application.
