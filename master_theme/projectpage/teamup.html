YORK U HACKS @YORK U

Background

" I am here to provoke you! ", said the opening speaker. YORK U HACKS (2018) was the first hackathon held at York University at it was all about bringing change to the society. The main speaker came through with a mind-opening speech, motivating us to work on ideas and solve problems that brought a positive impact to society and not just ideas that lead to personal monetary gain.

Our approach

Every community has a great amount of talent and dreams and insightful ideas, but sometimes they are laid to waste. As a team we decided to build a web based application, that served as a platform for local people could "TEAM UP" to share and work on ideas that would lead to positive change towards their society. For instance, building basketball courts in a neighbourhood.

Using Angular Web Framework, I was able to serve a role in building the frontend for this application.

Technologies used: Angular Web Framework, Typescript

Special thanks to my incredible team members Khizzar Sidiqui, and Sam Cymbaluk.
